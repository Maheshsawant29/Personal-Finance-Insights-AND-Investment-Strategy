# Financial Insights and Investment Strategy

## About the Study
This repository contains a study-based research report developed from my personal learning and understanding of finance and investment fundamentals.

The report focuses on inflation, earning capacity, investment strategy, asset allocation, risk management, and long-term wealth creation.

## Key Topics Covered
- Inflation
- Purpose of Investing
- Necessity of investment over savings
- Earning-first investment philosophy
- Equity, mutual funds, debt funds, Commodities(Gold, Silver) and Real Estate
- Risk management and diversification
- Emergency fund and three-bucket strategy
- Personal learnings and long-term mindset
